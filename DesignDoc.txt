Design Approach For Each Page


THEME - Restaurant Menu Book

Each page is a Menu page, the whole website is a Menu Book on a table.

The font will be this - https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Great+Vibes?lang=fod_Latn ( as this give very restaurant type vibe to me atleast)

Text  and icons will be in golden color , and background(of menu) will be black(or dark shades).

All the buttons are represented through icons ( as menu's have images on which stand out).

Transition of pages will be represented by page flipping animation.
